<space><space>+            <space>              double spaces to single space

\r\n\r\n                   \r\n                 delete blank lines (Windows)
^<space>+                  [blank]              trim leading whitespace
+$                         [blank]              trim trailing whitespace
^(.+)$                     <p>\1</p>            wrap <p> tags on every line
<p>(\*+*)</p>              <p class="<someclass>">\1</p>    add a class to all <p> tags
<img src="(.*?)" />        <img src="\1" alt="My alt text" />   Add alt text to images
&(?![a-zA-Z0-9]+?;)        &amp;               replace all & with &amp;
